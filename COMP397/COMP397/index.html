Investigate large object under the sheet

As you remove the sheet, you notice it is a machinery object with some dials, numbers that seemed to be represented in a date format. As you investigate the machine further, you discover a number pad. You come to the conclusion that the machine is infact a Time Machine. You are mind-blown. You decide to input some numbers into the number pad.

Do you choose to go to the ancient past, or the leading future?

Time travel to the Past

or

Time travel to the Future

Travel to the Past

You decide to travel into the past. As the doors close in the machine, you get a churning, sick feeling in your stomach, and your knees weaken. You pass out... As you start to regain conscious, you slowly get up from the floor of the Time machine and open the doors shakily.

You suddenly hear loud roars and screeches; you realize the Time Machine has brought you back to the Jurassic Age. While walking away from the Time Machine, you look around, and finally, into the sky. A falling meteor is hurling straight towards you.

You stumble out of the Time Machine, looking around in awe. You wonder how far into the future you have travelled. While you looked around, you notice flying cars zooming by. Everything has futuristic look, and you are captivated by its baffling aspects.
